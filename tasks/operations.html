Operands
Assignment
Assign a value to a variable
let x = 10;
Addition
Add two numbers together
let x = 10 + 5;
Subtraction
Subtract two numbers
let x = 10 - 5;
Multiplication
Multipliply two numbers together
let x = 10 * 5;
Division
Divide two numbers
let x = 10 / 5;
Exponential
Equivalent to Math.pow
let x = 10 ** 3; // 10*10*10
Increment
Increases the value by 1
let x = 10; x++;
Decrement
Decreases the value by 1
let x = 10; x--;
